This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2024-12-23 by and between Noor Okoro ("Client") and

disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Neither party's total liability shall exceed the Fee Cap set forth in Section 2.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party shall indemnify the other for third-party claims, subject to the limitation of liability in Section 4.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.
