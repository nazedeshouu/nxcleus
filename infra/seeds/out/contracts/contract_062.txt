This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2022-02-27 by and between Vertex Labs LLC ("Client") and

disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party shall indemnify the other without cap and irrespective of any other provision.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.
