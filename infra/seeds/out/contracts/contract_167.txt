MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2022-03-14 by and between Orbit Holdings LLC ("Client") and
Nexa Systems LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 24 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
Upon expiry, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 24-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 371,818 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the twelve months preceding the claim.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party indemnifies the other for third-party claims, but only to the extent of the capped liability above.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
