MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2023-03-09 by and between Kestrel Systems LLC ("Client") and
Lumen Holdings LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 12 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
Upon expiry, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 12-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days before the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 88,652 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Total liability under this Agreement shall be limited to USD 200,000 in the aggregate.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party shall indemnify the other solely up to the amounts permitted under the limitation of liability.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
