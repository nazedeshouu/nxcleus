MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2024-12-09 by and between Andrei Larsson ("Client") and
Nexa Holdings LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 36 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
Upon expiry, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 36-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 45 days before the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 257,706 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Total liability under this Agreement shall be limited to USD 800,000 in the aggregate.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Indemnification hereunder shall not exceed the aggregate liability cap set forth in Section 4.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
