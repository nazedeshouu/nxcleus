MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2022-04-01 by and between Vertex Group LLC ("Client") and
Nexa Systems LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 24 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
This Agreement does not renew automatically and expires at the end of the initial term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 284,785 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Neither party's total liability shall exceed the Fee Cap set forth in Section 2.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party indemnifies the other for third-party claims, but only to the extent of the capped liability above.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
