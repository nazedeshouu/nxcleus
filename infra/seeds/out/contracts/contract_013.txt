MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2022-04-19 by and between Vertex Group LLC ("Client") and
Orbit Group LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 36 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
Upon expiry, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 36-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days before the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 339,779 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. The maximum aggregate liability of either party is limited to the fees actually paid under this Agreement.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party shall indemnify the other solely up to the amounts permitted under the limitation of liability.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
