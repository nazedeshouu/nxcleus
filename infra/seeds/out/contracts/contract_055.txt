MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 2025-02-25 by and between Kestrel Group LLC ("Client") and
Nexa Partners LLC ("Provider").

1. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is 24 months, commencing on the Effective Date.
Upon expiry, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 24-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days before the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES. Provider shall invoice Client monthly. Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed USD 50,238 (the 'Fee Cap').

3. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information and shall not
disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the twelve months preceding the claim.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Indemnification hereunder shall not exceed the aggregate liability cap set forth in Section 4.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach upon 30 days' written notice if the
breach remains uncured.

7. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
