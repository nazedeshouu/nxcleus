How it works

Eight stages. One boundary crossing.

A described process flows through the pipeline once. Only stage 1 leaves the building, and it only ever sees a sanitized brief.

STAGE 0 · LOCAL

Intake & trust layer

Your policy plus a PII firewall mask and seal every identifier and document.

STAGE 1 · THE ONE EXTERNAL CALL

Frontier planning

A frontier model sees only the sanitized brief and designs the work topology plus the model bill-of-materials.

STAGE 2 · LOCAL

Certify & rehydrate

A local model checks the plan against the raw context the frontier never saw, and pins the goal.

STAGE 3 · LOCAL

Quote

A deterministic price from the model bill-of-materials, before a single build token is spent.

STAGE 4 · LOCAL FLEET

Wave build

A parallel fleet of specialized models builds in waves, a conductor reviewing between each.

STAGE 5 · LOCAL

Consolidate

Wave outputs merge into one coherent process, gated by objective tests.

STAGE 6 · LOCAL

Adversarial QA

Inspectors attack the build; a lineage-independent numeric oracle re-derives the math; the goal is re-checked.

STAGE 7 · LOCAL

Operations registry

Lands as a versioned asset: runnable forever, metered per run, refinable without starting over.
