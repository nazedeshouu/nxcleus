Sovereign Mode

Functional presenter-gated kill-switch · api.setSovereign() wired
Zero non-local dispatchLocal

When engaged, even the stage-1 planner call is pinned to local:B/glm-46 — the platform dispatches nothing off-fleet. A planner call attempted under sovereign against an EXTERNAL binding fails closed with SovereignViolation (the demo proves this by showing the red egress row).

Global default toggle here; per-job override lives on the Build intake. Applies from the next dispatch, not retroactively.
