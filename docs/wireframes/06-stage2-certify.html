Goal statement D10

Functional
What must exist when this job is done · read-only

An operator can submit a new applicant — legal name, government ID, date of birth, residential address, and declared source of funds — and the process must screen them against the OFAC SDN and EU consolidated sanctions lists and a PEP dataset, compute a risk score from the declared weights with banker's rounding, assign a RED / AMBER / GREEN band, and emit a reviewer-ready decision record citing every hit. Anything AMBER or above is queued for human review, never auto-cleared.

derived from the original raw request + certified plan · persisted jobs.goal · event certify.goal_set
6
Read-only by design (D10) — carried on the job, pinned on the Build view, and consumed by the conductor (stage 4), the goal-fulfillment check (stage 6) and the package manifest (stage 7). Events carry it, so Functional; also readable via GET /jobs/{id} and GET /jobs/{id}/plan. No dedicated goal endpoint — no edit control exists.
