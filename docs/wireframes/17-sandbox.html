Meridian Bank — Terms of Sensitive Data Use

These terms govern any automated processing of Bank Records within the Nxcleus data boundary. All customer, account, and transaction records are classified RAW and are confined to the LOCAL zone; no raw field may cross to an external model under any circumstance.

Permitted purposes
  • Sanctions and watchlist screening against OFAC and EU consolidated lists.
  • Anti-money-laundering pattern detection (structuring, layering, dormant-account reactivation).
  • Fraud and anomaly review across accounts and transactions.
Boundary obligations
  • Only a sanitized brief — with names, account numbers, and identifiers removed — may be emitted to a frontier planner.
  • Distilled detection policy must preserve customer confidentiality; the certifier holds the only RAW-access grant.

Fetched by GET /sandbox/companies/{id}/terms ({markdown, seeded}). If seeded=false: "This company hasn't published its terms of data use yet." No terms → no fabricated policy.
