## How to participate
1. Click **Enroll / Sign up with AMD** on the [event page](https://lablab.ai/ai-hackathons/amd-developer-hackathon-act-ii/).
2. Create / sign in to the **AMD AI Developer Program (ADP)** — required for credits and approval.
3. Wait for enrollment approval, then access GPUs and credits.
4. Build solo or form a team via the lablab Discord.

## Who can join & teams
- **Anyone** with a passion for AI. Sign up on lablab.ai + register for ADP.
- No team yet? Find teammates on the **[lablab Discord](https://discord.gg/lablabai)**.
